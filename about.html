About Paws & Whiskers

Passionate pet people sharing what we know.

Our Mission

At Paws & Whiskers, we believe every pet deserves a life filled with love, proper care, and understanding. Our mission is to provide pet owners — whether first-timers or seasoned veterans — with reliable, research-backed advice that makes a real difference in the lives of animals.

We cover everything from nutrition and health to behavior and enrichment, always with the animal's well-being at the heart of every article.

Who We Are

We are a small team of pet enthusiasts, veterinary professionals, and experienced pet owners who have come together to create a trusted resource for the global pet community. Between us, we've cared for dogs, cats, birds, hamsters, rabbits, fish, and even the occasional hedgehog.

Every article on this site is researched, fact-checked, and written with the goal of helping you become the best pet parent you can be.

Our Values

🐾 Compassion first. We promote humane, force-free care methods and strongly support adoption over buying.

🔬 Science-based. Our advice is grounded in current veterinary science and animal behavior research.

🌍 Accessible to all. We explain complex topics in plain language so every pet owner can benefit.
